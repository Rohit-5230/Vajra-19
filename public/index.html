ABOUT US







We at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, have always upheld sportsmanship in its highest spirits and have strived to make sure people with a passion for sports have access to a plethora of sports with modern facilities. It was in this motivation that we came up with the idea of VAJRA: which in essence is symbolic of strength, athleticism and a never die attitude that underlines the character of a true sportsman. The objective of the countrywide sports extravaganza is to assemble like-minded sportsmen and provide them with a platform to showcase their talent, get recognized and realize their true passion for the sport in a highly competitive, non-bias environment.
