ABOUT US


"Vajra", the riveting Sports fest of BIT Mesra aims at developing a spirit of sportsmanship and provides a platform to test the skill and resilience of athletes.

It is always dedicatedly worked upon to be the absolute pinnacle in intercollege sporting events. BIT Mesra through Vajra is known for its grit for sports, state of the art facilities, ethical standards and top notch hospitality & accomodation. We aim to set the bar higher every year in all the above departments.

Now Vajra is back and this 3 day sports extravaganza is scheduled from November 8-10. We will be looking forward to your participation in huge numbers and go beyond in our efforts to eclipse the previous editions!
